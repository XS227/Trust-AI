From share to order

A simple, automated process — from the first referral to commission paid out.

2

3

4

5

6

Paid out

Frequently asked questions

Is TrustAi an affiliate solution?

Yes, but with a focus on quality, attribution and control. You own the data, we track the orders, and you set the rules. No affiliate networks in between taking high commissions.

Does the store pay before any sales?

No. The model is performance-based — commission is only paid out when actual orders are completed and paid. You don't pay for empty clicks.

Does it work with Shopify?

Yes — TrustAi can be installed as a lightweight theme snippet, and orders are tracked automatically via Shopify webhook (orders/create). No complex integrations.

What about WooCommerce and Wix?

WooCommerce and Wix integrations are coming soon. For now, you can use the API directly for custom solutions.

How do you prevent abuse?

TrustAi has built-in IP tracking, attribution windows (24h–30 days), and self-purchase detection. You have full control over rules and can approve ambassadors before they are activated.

30-day pilot. We set it up together — Shopify installation, ambassador onboarding, first commission model. You measure results before committing further.
