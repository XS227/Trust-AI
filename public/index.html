How it works

From merchant onboarding to first sale.

Set up a campaign, invite ambassadors, distribute unique links and let the platform track every click, lead and conversion.

1

Merchant onboarding

Connect your Shopify store. TrustAI installs webhooks and is ready to track orders in minutes.

2

Create a campaign

Define what counts as a result: a sale, lead, booking, application or custom event.

3

Invite ambassadors

Ambassadors apply through a branded signup flow. You review and approve from the dashboard.

4

Track every referral

Unique links connect every click, lead and sale back to the right ambassador automatically.

5

Pay on real outcomes

Commission is calculated per confirmed order. Performance data is live in both dashboards.
