Frequently asked questions

Does TrustAi replace our CRM?

No. TrustAi sits in front of the CRM and captures partner introductions before they become leads. You can connect TrustAi to HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive or other systems for full pipeline visibility.

How is commission calculated?

You set the commission model yourself — fixed amount, percentage of deal value, or tiered (more for larger deals). TrustAi tracks attribution automatically and calculates payouts.

What about attribution over long sales cycles?

TrustAi supports attribution windows of up to 12 months, with first-click or last-click models. You choose what fits your industry.

Can partners see their own pipeline?

Yes. Each partner gets their own dashboard showing clicks, leads, pipeline status and earned commission — in real time. This significantly increases engagement.
