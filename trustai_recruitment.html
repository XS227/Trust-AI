Frequently asked questions

Does TrustAi replace an ATS?

No. TrustAi is designed for the sourcing layer before or alongside the ATS. It helps you create and measure better candidate intake.

How are candidates tracked?

Each person or partner gets a unique link. When a candidate registers interest, they are linked to the right source and can be followed through the process.

Can this be used with existing recruitment systems?

Yes. It can be used as a separate entry for referral candidates, and data can later be linked against existing processes or systems.
